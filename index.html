FULL MOON RESTAURANT










Inspired by the English country garden, our restaurant offers a comfortable place for guests to come eat, drink and relax. The outside courtyard booths has been fitted with reclaimed timber screens to enable social distancing with the Orangery and dining room providing beautiful spaces for coffee, lunch, drinks and dinner. Our fantastic team, many of whom have been with us since the beginning of our journey, are ready to great you with that famous Saigon smile, welcoming you to share the sunset on The Deck and unwind from your busy schedules.
