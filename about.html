Our fantastic team, many of whom have been with us since the beginning of our journey, are ready to great you with that famous Saigon smile, welcoming you to share the sunset on The Deck and unwind from your busy schedules. Our bar, is a chilled-out place to catch up with friends and unwind after work.
Our bartenders, are always on hand to welcome guests and to mix your favourite drink.The Deck Saigon has a notable wine list representing some of the world’s major vineyards and fashionable wines. Our Grand Cru selection for the more discerning palate is extensive, with something for experts and novices alike.





Within The Dining Room, an intimate 28-seat fine dining restaurant helmed by ten-time Michelin-starred Executive Chef Jared Wentworth, guests are treated to a hyper-seasonal, refined tasting menu alongside specialty beer pairings curated by Brewmaster Jared Rouben.

In a polished yet warm, minimalist setting, Wentworth’s signature fare intersects with Rouben’s innovative pairings through the manipulation of temperature, quantity, verticals, tinctures and more — highlighting both the culinary ingredients used in creating each dish as well as Moody Tongue’s culinary brewing techniques.
